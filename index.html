Good Charlotte

Good Charlotte started their band in 1995. The lead singer is Joel Madden, Billy Martin plays lead guitar, Benji Madden plays backup guitar, Paul Thomas plays bass, and Dean Butterworth plays the drums. Their genre can be described as ealry 2000s punk alternative. One of their most popular albums was "The Young and the Hopeless," selling 3.5 million copies.
